About Splash!

Splash! is a company based in Fresno, California. Formed in 2019, Splash! is an online marketplace which lets people rent out their properties or spare areas in the land to guests. Splash! provides access to 1+ million unique places to stay in more than 5,000 cities. With Splash!'s people-to-people platform benefits all its stakeholders, including hosts, guests, employees and the communities in which it operates.
